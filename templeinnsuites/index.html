The Ogden Temple

The Ogden Utah temple was closed for 3 1/2 years to undergo renovations that significantly modified the look of the building. Following an open house from Aug. 1 - Sep. 6, 2014, the temple was rededicated on September 21, 2014. It is located on 350 22nd St. Ogden, Utah and is open for appointments and walk-ins.
